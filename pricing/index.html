Three engines, one app

SpeakWith picks the right tool for the job. Here's what each engine is great at.

Free & Pro

Apple

Built into macOS. Instant, offline, and free. Perfect for quick dictation and everyday notes.

Pro

Whisper

Maximum robustness on tough audio: accents, background noise, crosstalk. Steps up from a fast model to a high-accuracy model when it senses lower confidence.

Automatic escalation requires the optional accuracy add-on.
Pro

Parakeet

The speed champion. Near-instant transcription with excellent accuracy on clean audio.
